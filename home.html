WHAT IS TRAINING PROVIDER MANAGEMENT SYSTEM?

A Training Provider Management System (TPMS) is a software or digital platform created to support organisations in efficiently managing and coordinating their training programs and activities. It functions as a centralised system that simplifies multiple tasks associated with training providers and courses. This platform empowers students to register for courses at their convenience while ensuring they receive top-quality training from designated instructors.

HOW CAN WE HELP YOU?

  • Online Courses

    • TPMS provides access to thousands of online courses covering various subjects and disciplines. These courses are developed and taught by leading universities and institutions from around the world.

  • Specialisations

    • TPMS offers curated sequences of courses called Specializations. Specialisations allow learners to gain in-depth knowledge and skills in a specific subject area by completing a series of related courses.

  • Professional Certificates

    • TPMS provides Professional Certificate programs that are designed to enhance specific job skills. These programs are developed in collaboration with industry experts and top universities, and they focus on practical skills and real-world applications.

  • Community and Learning Support

    • TPMS fosters a learning community through discussion forums, peer feedback, and interactive assignments. Learners can collaborate with peers, ask questions, and receive support from instructors and the Coursera community.
